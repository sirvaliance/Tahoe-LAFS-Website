Tahoe-LAFS

Open, distributed, and secure cloud storage system.
Tahoe-LAFS is a Free Software/Open Source storage technology.
Your data is distributed across multiple servers. Even if some of the servers fail or are taken over by an attacker, the entire filesystem continues to function correctly.
Your data is encrypted and the service provider never has the ability to read or modify your data.
Your data is accessible from many clients, including the web browser, command line, Javascript frontends, Windows virtual drive, Tahoe backup tool, Duplicity, and (S)FTP clients.
